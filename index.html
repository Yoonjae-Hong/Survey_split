Survey on Housing Needs and Ideal Floor Plan Design

This study aims to explore how people describe their ideal home layout and how these preferences can be represented through spatial design. Your responses—including textual descriptions and a simple drag-and-drop floor-plan task—will help us better understand individual housing needs and spatial reasoning.


The survey includes several sections covering:

  • Basic background information
  • Your housing preferences
  • A detailed description of your ideal one-story home
  • A floor-plan creation task using the tool on the right side

Please answer each question sincerely and thoughtfully. There are no right or wrong answers—only your personal preferences. The entire survey will take approximately 20-25 minutes.


All responses will remain anonymous. No personally identifiable information will be collected. The data will be used solely for academic research.
